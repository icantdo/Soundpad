SoundPad

How to use:
  • Click sound buttons to play (visualizer appears automatically)
  • Adjust volume slider for each sound (0-100%)
  • Check "Loop" to repeat a sound continuously
  • Stop individual sounds from "Now Playing" section
  • Organize sounds: Create folders in 'sounds' directory (e.g., 'sounds/music/', 'sounds/effects/')
